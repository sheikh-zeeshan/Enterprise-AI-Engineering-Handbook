# 4.15 Enterprise Best Practices

Recommendations include:

* Establish AI engineering standards before scaling.
* Separate governance from implementation.
* Standardize prompts, instructions, and reusable skills.
* Integrate AI into existing SDLC rather than replacing it.
* Measure quality alongside productivity.
* Keep humans accountable for architectural decisions.
* Design workflows to remain portable across AI platforms.

---

# 4.16 Common Anti-Patterns

Examples include:

* Treating AI as an infallible developer
* Allowing every developer to invent personal standards
* Ignoring architectural governance
* Measuring only speed instead of quality
* Embedding vendor-specific assumptions into core engineering practices
* Skipping human review for production changes
